I'm a freelance web developer looking to transition into product management. I've led a team of programming interns at RCMakes, a high school makerspace and entrepreneurshop program. I also developed and piloted new software development classes for the Career Education Center (CEC) of Ventura County.

My tech journey began in 2017 when a friend gave me Hackers & Painters by Y Combinator co-founder Paul Graham. The essays on hacking and business were insightful and eye-opening, and I knew from that point I wanted to work in the field.
When I'm not at my computer, you'll find me outside - sailing and surfing.
